About Me

I originally studied Computer Science at Hawaii Pacific University while stationed there with my Air Force father, but switched my major a few times to Mathematics and then Physics. As a result, I have a great background working with advanced math and physics equations/expressions. I've transferred credits over to Bellevue College and just started taking classes here this Summer 2017 to complete the degree I originally started.

My goal is to continue over the next year at Bellevue College and get my Associate's Degree in Computer Information Systems, via the Software Development track. I hope to eventually work for one of the many amazing local companies here in Washington state.
